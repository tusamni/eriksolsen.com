### Lipstick 911

A local Porsche collector contacted me to help list this beautiful narrow-body, mid year "S". The mid-year market has always lagged behind, due to relibility concerns, impact bumpers and emissions equipment.

That said, this is a stunning example with its light-red interior. It's a near perfect drivers mid-year and I'd be happy to drive it every day. Even five years later, this is still the fourth highest result for a 1977 Targa. It's a testament to this cars restoration and options.
